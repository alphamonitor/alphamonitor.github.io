ALpha Monitor



An Aid and a Solution for Alzheimer's Patients



Problems Faced by Alzheimer's Patients

  • World wide diseases like Alzheimer’s / Dementia affect more than 30 Million people every year.

  • Wandering is one of the biggest problems affecting Alzheimer’s Patients. They wander at abnormal timings without the notice of the caregiver.

  • Patients also face Irregular Health Checkups due to various reasons.



Features of the ALpha Monitor

• Monitors Wandering, Pulse and Body Temperature of Patient.

• Alerts Caregiver if Patient’s Body Conditions are abnormal.

• Health Status of Patient is displayed in a Mobile App.

• Health Status is also sent to the Doctor as Daily Reports through SMS.

• A camera monitors Alzheimer’s Patient’s Physical Aggression and Fall detection.

• Medicine Reminder reminds Patients to take medicines at right time.

• Algorithm Predicts Wandering with Machine Learning Time.
